# I, Me, My, Mine and Myself
I’ve been an engineer since I was four years old. I used to sit in my father’s
workshop, cutting wheels out of plywood with a jigsaw for a little Unimog truck
I wanted to build. The fate of that project remains unrecorded.

Later, I studied Computer Engineering in Hamburg and made software development
my career. Most recently, I led a software development team of more than 100
people. I’m now a man of leisure (the word ‘pensioner’ doesn’t really suit me).
Occasionally, I advise companies run by friends when they’re looking for help
with challenging web and AI-based software projects.

»Rough edges« and »awesome shit« have always fascinated me. Now I finally have
the time to pursue my hobbies: Cycling, home automation, and fiNe-scale model
building (the capital 'N' is a nod to N scale).

This website exists only to share things with friends and colleagues who
are interested in them.
